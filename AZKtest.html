Adzuki is the name of my millipede [named after the adzuki bean]. Millipedes like a fairly constant temperature of around 21 -24 deg C, and 70-80% Humidity. And what better way to provide this than with an automated vivarium. I have built it all inside of this old CRT television set, and control it with the modest RPi 02w
