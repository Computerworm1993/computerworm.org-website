Introduction: On boot my main menu script opens. Navigated by channel up and down on the remote to cycle through the different programs. Pressing enter will open the selected script and then the newly opened script will terminate the main menu. Power button from any of my programs will do the reverse and relaunch the main menu program. This keeps things from getting bogged down with too much running at once and allows me to keep all my programs neat and tidy in there own seperate files. Opened and closed as needed.
